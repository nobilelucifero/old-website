I spent most of my time working with the App Team. Fortunately, I had the chance to contribute with many other teams to a variety of projects and initiatives. This page represents an overview of my experience at Zalando.

Highlight My experience in the app team

Used by millions of customers every month, the Zalando App is one of the top performing products within the Zalando Platform. Pretty much in any possible metric.

Within the first weeks after I joined, things in the team changed a lot: two long-time members of the design team went off to other projects in the organization, all while the development team was getting restructured and an important release was getting developed and Q&A‚Äôd.

My first tasks were porting a large portion of the Phone UI to Tablet (that had a legacy interface) and starting the creation of a Component Library for the design and dev team to use.

Ultimately, my role as a Designer is to first and foremost care about the product and the customer. Every initiative I carry on is revolving around that. Be it shipping a whimsical UI transition, structure a user research plan, getting the team to release more often, or even empowering developers to make design decisions.

I am more and more of a believer that Design is something everybody can participate to.
With this idea in mind, what I can do as a designer is facilitate the design process, collaborate with teams, and advocate for the user at hand.
