Seilinee (Reallly)

Even for this experience, I refer more to my CV. The whole project consisted in creating a redistributable Property Manager for Real Estates and they clients.

The project started with as a work for a specific client but with already the idea of selling it in partnership and sponsorship with him and other private partners.

For this project, I worked on literally everything. From the very first logic sketches, going through detailed wireframes and mockups to being given later to an external development team located abroad.
I later helped the developers implementing interactions, other UI elements and few touches of RWD where needed.

I also produced a fairly big amount of printed materials, but they're mostly related to the original client.
