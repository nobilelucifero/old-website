Sometimes—in order to come up with the right ideas—you have to ask yourself the right questions. This is what I did while actually working on the following sketches and drawings. Key questions I came up with for myself are:

  • What's the temporal dimension of this Product?
  • How are people supposed to interact and share content? (personal or not)
  • Is the Card creation an encouraged process? Why? How?
  • Do we care about creating a personal network of contacts? Or are these Cards supposed to not last long?

I can't really reply to these questions yet, but it's anyway interesting to start already pondering about these themes.

First Board: Business Cards Shapes


From now on, I'm going to reference Business Cards as Glitter Cards or simply Cards.

Cards! They can be shaped in so many ways… in the preparatory sketches they started as plain 'ol Business Cards, becoming at the end polygons or even 3D objects.
Unfortunately, I couldn't really organize the AI of these Cards in a meaningful or practical way, therefore they didn't make it in the final drawings.

So, after trying a couple more shapes, I dediced to stick with rectangles or squares. Honestly, the "portrait" Cards I see in Glitter Alpha already pleases me (although sizes and proportions could be different).

Second Board: Business Cards Systems


Fitting these Cards within a System, Grid or Hieracy is not an easy task. I ended up imagining Glitter as a Inbox of incoming contacts, in which its temporal dimension is (relatively) short.

Mainly, I see 3 to 4 main Sections useful to the App's purposes.

You (Profile)

I believe a spotlight of your own Card is always very helpful. The Business Card I will share the most is definitely my own one!

Inbox

This is most transitional Section. Incoming, fresh, Cards that are not sorted or archived are going to end up here.

From Your Networks (a.k.a. Discovery)

Probably the best way to engage users in the App is to let them discover possible now or already knows Contacts, or rather, a way to connect with them.
I can easily think about Homescreen App. Such a simple Discovery, yet amazingly satisfactory! ;)

Stored Contacts

I'm not very sure we would need such section in the main screen of the App, but I imagine it being useful when you want to share saved or archived Cards to others.
My fundamental concern here is privacy, but I'm probably overthinking about it already.

This was probably another fun part: I enjoyed it a lot! Writing some quick User Stories mafe me derive the most interesting ideas for this project, possibly.

Sharing has to be effective, fast and friction-less (probably also fun) to make this App successful. I definitely believe it's a result definitely possible to achieve.

In particular, Sharing Channels (the way you share) could dictate the way people interact with Glitter. I particularly liked to ability to share via SMS + deeplink (fast, cheap & easy). There must be though alternative ways to do it otherwise.
I quickly tried to outline the most common ones (without being sci-fi about it).

In this Board I also got the idea of archiving. Which made me ask myself: how much these Cards are supposed to last in Glitter or in your Phone? Can Glitter become an extension of your Contacts App?

Last but not least: funny features! How about having the ability to create your own persona in identikit-style to make people interact a bit more with the App? Just an idea here. It might be only for avatars that accompany the Cards. :)

I believe this only a tiny starting point… many other ideas flooded my mind, but I decided to put down on paper only few of them.
