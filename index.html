How I like to ship successful products 👇

Define

Defining the business needs, vision, problems. This is where you set your first goals.

Understand

It's users and data here. Gather insights on your product and customers (or future ones!).

Create

Designing a better user behavior change that meets both the business and user needs.

Validate

Identifying and testing assumptions is critical in order to avoid later failure, at high costs.

Measure

Measure the impact of your changes to understand if your product is succeeding.

Iterate

This is where it gets fun! Identify the next iteration and start learning all over again. 🙌
